Digital Banner

Problem Statement

You have been approached by a travel company, Travelly, to produce digital banners for their company’s web and social media site. The client has prepared several photos for you to work on for the creation of the banner.

Using the images provided, you have to create:

  1. Instagram grid banner
    • Split (3 x 3) Instagram grid to gain users attention for their upcoming promotion
  2. Digital banner
    • Collage photo that represents the company services
Either web site banner or the social media post must include the photo of the man emerging out of a torn paper background provided.

You realize that, to achieve the natural effects of a photo collage, several elements must be emphasized, such as the colour and atmosphere of the environment.

He has asked you to find more images of the related scenes and produce the desired effects by the end of the day. He is also agreeable to let you inject other elements to enhance the overall mood. Creativity and originality is the key for this job.

Get ready to fly your banners and show your Instagram grid post!
